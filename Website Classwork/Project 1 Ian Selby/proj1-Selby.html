Comparison of starter racial buffs in D&D

Dragonborn
They have +2 strength, +1 Charisma, Draconic Ancestry, Breathe Weapon and Damage Resistance
Dwarf
They have +2 constitution, Darkvision, dwarven resistance, dwarven combat training and stonecutting
Elf
They have +2 Dexterity, Darkvision, Keen Senses, Fey Ancestry and Trance
Gnome
They have +2 Intelligence, Darkvision and Gnome Cunning
Half-Elf
They have +2 Charisma, +1 to 2 player chosen stats, darkvision, fey ancestry and skill versatility
Halfling
They have +2 Dexterity, Lucky, Brave and halfling nimbleness
Half-Orc
They have +2 strength, +1 constitution, Darkvision, menacing, relentless endurance and savage attacks
Human
They have +1 to all ability scores and extra language
Tiefling
They have +2 charisma, +1 intelligence, darkvision, hellish resistance and infernal legacy
